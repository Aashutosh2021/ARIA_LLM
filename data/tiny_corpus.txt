Once upon a time there was a little cat. The cat was small and happy.
The cat liked to play in the sun. Every day the cat would run and jump.
One day the cat saw a red ball. The cat ran to the ball and played with it.
The little cat was very happy with the red ball. The cat played all day long.

Once upon a time there was a small dog. The dog was big and kind.
The dog liked to run in the park. Every day the dog would walk and play.
One day the dog saw a blue toy. The dog ran to the toy and played with it.
The kind dog was very happy with the blue toy. The dog played all day long.

Once upon a time there was a young bird. The bird was little and sweet.
The bird liked to sing in the tree. Every day the bird would fly and sing.
One day the bird saw a green leaf. The bird flew to the leaf and sat on it.
The sweet bird was very happy in the tall tree. The bird sang all day long.

The sun was warm and the sky was blue. The little cat and the small dog were friends.
They liked to play together in the park. The cat would run and the dog would jump.
The young bird would sing a happy song. All the animals were happy in the sun.

Once upon a time the cat and the dog and the bird went for a walk.
They walked to the big tree by the river. The water was cool and clear.
The cat looked at the water. The dog looked at the tree. The bird looked at the sky.
They were happy to be friends. They played together every day in the warm sun.

The end of the little story about the cat and the dog and the sweet little bird.
